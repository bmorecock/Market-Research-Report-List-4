Modular robots are made up of individual modules that can be reconfigured for different tasks. Humanoid robots are designed to resemble the human body, while Swarm Robotics involves coordinating large numbers of simple robots to work together. Soft robots are made of flexible materials, allowing for unique movement capabilities. Other types of bio-inspired robots may include those inspired by animals or plants, such as fish robots or plant-inspired robots.</p></p>
