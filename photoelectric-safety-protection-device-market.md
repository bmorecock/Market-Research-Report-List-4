<p><ul><li>PNP Output</li><li>NPN Output</li><li>OSE Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoelectric safety protection devices come in various types, including PNP output, NPN output, and OSE output. PNP output devices provide a positive signal output when an object is detected, while NPN output devices provide a negative signal output. OSE output devices combine both PNP and NPN outputs in a single sensor, offering versatile signal outputs. Each type caters to different applications and preferences, making them suitable for a wide range of industries requiring reliable and efficient safety protection devices.</p></p>
