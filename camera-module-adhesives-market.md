<p><p>Camera Module Adhesives market consists of two main types: UV Curable Type and Non UV Curable Type. UV Curable adhesives are cured using ultraviolet light, offering fast curing times and high bond strength. On the other hand, Non UV Curable adhesives do not require UV light for curing. These adhesives are suitable for applications where UV light is not accessible. Both types offer strong bonding capability and are widely used in the manufacturing of camera modules for various electronic devices.</p></p>
