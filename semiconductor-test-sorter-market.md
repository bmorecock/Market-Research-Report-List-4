<p><ul><li>Gravity-Feed Test Sorter</li><li>Turret Test Sorter</li><li>Pick-and-Place Test Sorter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor test sorter market includes three main types: Gravity-Feed Test Sorters, Turret Test Sorters, and Pick-and-Place Test Sorters. Gravity-Feed Test Sorters use gravity to feed devices onto the test handler, suitable for high-volume production. Turret Test Sorters have a rotating turret to transfer devices to the handler, ideal for multi-test and complex operations. Pick-and-Place Test Sorters utilize robotic arms to pick and place devices for testing, offering high flexibility and precision. Each type caters to different requirements and applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
