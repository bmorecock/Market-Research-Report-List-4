<p><p>Oriented Strand Board (OSB) subflooring is a type of engineered wood product commonly used in construction for flooring applications. OSB subflooring provides a strong, durable, and cost-effective option for supporting finished flooring materials in residential and commercial buildings.</p><p>The Oriented Strand Board Subflooring Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.50% during the forecast period. OSB subflooring is preferred by builders and contractors due to its strength, dimensional stability, and resistance to moisture.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the OSB subflooring market include the development of moisture-resistant and fire-retardant varieties of OSB, as well as the increasing adoption of prefabricated subflooring systems for faster installation.
