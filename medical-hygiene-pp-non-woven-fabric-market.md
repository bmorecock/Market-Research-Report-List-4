<p><p>Medical & Hygiene PP Non Woven Fabric market comprises of various types such as Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid. Drylaid method involves laying down fibers and bonding them through heat or chemicals. Airlaid method uses air to form a web of fibers which is later bonded. Wetlaid method includes making a fiber slurry that is laid out and dried. Spunlaid method involves extruding fibers onto a conveyor belt and bonding them together. Each type serves different purposes in the medical and hygiene industries.</p></p>
