<p><p>Low Voltage Vacuum Contractor Market types 1P, 2P, 3P, 4P, 5P categorize the number of poles in the contactor. 1P refers to contractors with one pole, suitable for single-phase systems. 2P means two poles, used for two-phase systems. 3P is for three-pole contactors fitting three-phase systems. 4P contactors have four poles, ideal for three-phase systems with a neutral wire. 5P contactors accommodate five poles, designed for specialized applications like grounding. Understanding these market types helps in selecting the right contactor for specific electrical systems.</p></p>
