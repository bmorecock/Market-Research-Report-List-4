<p><p>Milk fat fractions are categorized into three types based on their melting points. Low melting fractions have a melting point below room temperature, making them suitable for applications like spreads and ice creams. Medium melting fractions have a melting point around room temperature, ideal for bakery and confectionery products. High melting fractions have a melting point above room temperature, often used in chocolate and coatings. These different types cater to various food industries, offering versatility and functionality in product development.</p></p>
