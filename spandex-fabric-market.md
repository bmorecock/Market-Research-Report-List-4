<p><p>Spandex fabric market types include Solution Dry Spinning and Solution Wet Spinning. Solution Dry Spinning involves dissolving spandex chips in a solvent and spinning them into fibers through a dry spinning process. On the other hand, Solution Wet Spinning involves dissolving spandex chips in a liquid chemical solution and spinning them into fibers through a wet spinning process. Both methods result in high-quality spandex fabrics with stretch and recovery properties, catering to various industries such as activewear, lingerie, and hosiery.</p></p>
