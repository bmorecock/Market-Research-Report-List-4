<p><p>Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) cables are used to transmit power between the drive and motor in variable frequency drives. They come in different types such as Foil/Braid Shield, Copper Tape Shield, Armor Shield, and Others. Foil/Braid Shield cables have layers of foil and braided shielding for protection against electromagnetic interference. Copper Tape Shield cables have a shield made of copper tape for better flexibility and grounding. Armor Shield cables have an additional layer of armor for mechanical protection. Other types may include combinations of different shielding methods for specific applications.</p></p>
