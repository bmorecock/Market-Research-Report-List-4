Flame retardant type bubble film is designed to resist flames and reduce the risk of fire damage. Aluminized foil type bubble film offers enhanced insulation and reflection properties. Other types of bubble protective film include eco-friendly options, colored films, and heavy-duty versions for extra protection.</p></p>
