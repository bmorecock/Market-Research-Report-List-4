<p><p>Peelable lidding films can be categorized into different types based on the material they are made of. These types include Polyethylene (PE), Polyamide (PA), Polypropylene (PP), Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC), and Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET). Each type has its own unique properties and suitability for different applications. PE lidding films are known for their flexibility, while PA films offer excellent barrier properties. PP films are heat resistant, PVC films are transparent, and PET films are strong and durable.</p></p>
