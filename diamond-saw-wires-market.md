<p><p>Diamond saw wires are essential tools in cutting hard materials like concrete, stone, and metal. There are two main types of diamond saw wires in the market: electroplated wire and resin wire. Electroplated wires are coated with a layer of diamond particles through an electroplating process, making them ideal for cutting harder materials. On the other hand, resin wires use a resin bond that provides flexibility and durability, making them suitable for cutting softer materials while still delivering precise cuts.</p></p>
