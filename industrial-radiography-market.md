<p><p>Industrial radiography market comprises two main types: digital and film-based. Digital radiography utilizes digital sensors to capture and store images, providing clear and immediate results. It offers advantages such as faster image processing and storage, as well as the ability to enhance and manipulate images for better analysis. On the other hand, film-based radiography uses traditional film to capture images, which are then developed and examined manually. While digital radiography is gaining popularity due to its convenience and efficiency, film-based radiography is still widely used in certain industries.</p></p>
