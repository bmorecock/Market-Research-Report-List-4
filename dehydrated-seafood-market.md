<p><p>Dehydrated seafood comes in various types such as spray dried, freeze dried, vacuum dried, sun dried, hot air dried, and others. Spray drying involves using hot air to evaporate the moisture from the seafood. Freeze drying involves freezing the seafood and then removing the ice through sublimation. Vacuum drying removes moisture by placing the seafood in a vacuum chamber. Sun drying involves drying the seafood under the sun. Hot air drying uses hot air to remove moisture. Other methods may include microwave drying or infrared drying.</p></p>
