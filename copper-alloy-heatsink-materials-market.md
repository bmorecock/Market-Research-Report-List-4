<p><p>Copper alloy heatsink materials market types include Cu-Mo, Cu-Al, Cu-Zr, Cu-Fe, Cu-W, and others. Cu-Mo heatsinks contain copper and molybdenum, offering high thermal conductivity. Cu-Al heatsinks consist of copper and aluminum, providing good corrosion resistance and thermal conductivity. Cu-Zr heatsinks incorporate copper and zirconium for improved strength and thermal conductivity. Cu-Fe heatsinks contain copper and iron for enhanced thermal performance. Cu-W heatsinks combine copper and tungsten for increased hardness and thermal conductivity. Other variations also exist in the market, each offering unique properties for heat dissipation.</p></p>
