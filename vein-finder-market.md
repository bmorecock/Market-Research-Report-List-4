<p><p>Vein finders are medical devices used to locate veins for efficient venipuncture procedures. Display type vein finders have a screen that displays the vein map in real-time, aiding healthcare professionals in identifying the exact location of veins. Non-display type vein finders do not have a screen but use infrared technology to project a map of veins directly onto the patient's skin.
