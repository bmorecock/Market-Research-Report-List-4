<p><p>Flexi-bag market types include monolayer and multilayer options. Monolayer flexi-bags consist of a single layer of material, making them simple and cost-effective. They are commonly used for non-hazardous liquids. On the other hand, multilayer flexi-bags have several layers of material, providing added strength and protection. They are ideal for transporting hazardous or sensitive liquids that require extra security. Both monolayer and multilayer flexi-bags serve different purposes based on the needs of the product being transported.</p></p>
