<p><p>Veterinary ICU cages market is segmented into two types - Monoblock Type and Parallel Type. Monoblock Type cages are integrated units with connected compartments for animals, allowing for easy access and monitoring. Parallel Type cages are individual units placed side by side, suitable for larger animals or customized configurations. Both types offer features designed for critical care veterinary needs, such as adjustable shelves, waste management systems, and easy cleaning. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements and space available in the veterinary facility.</p></p>
