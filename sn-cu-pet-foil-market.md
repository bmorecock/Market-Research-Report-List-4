<p><p>Sn-Cu-PET foil market types include rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by rolling a thick copper slab through a series of rollers to achieve the desired thickness. It is commonly used in flexible printed circuits and electromagnetic shielding applications. Electrolytic copper foil is made by electrolysis of copper sulfate solution and is known for its high purity and conductivity. It is often used in high-end electronic products such as smartphones and laptops.</p></p>
