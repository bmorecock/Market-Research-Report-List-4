<p><p>Electric Overhead Traveling (EOT) Cranes are classified into different types based on their design and function. Single Girder Cranes have one beam that runs along the length of the building, while Double Girder Bridge Cranes have two beams for added support and lifting capacity. Gantry Cranes are versatile cranes that can be used indoors or outdoors, while Monorails are designed for straight line movement along a track. Each type of EOT crane offers unique benefits and is suited for specific lifting requirements in various industries.</p></p>
