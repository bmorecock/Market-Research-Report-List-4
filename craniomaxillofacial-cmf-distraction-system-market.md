<p><p>The Craniomaxillofacial (CMF) Distraction System market includes two main types: Multi-vector Distraction and Bi-directional Distraction. Multi-vector Distraction involves the simultaneous distraction in multiple axes, allowing for more precise adjustments and scaffolding. On the other hand, Bi-directional Distraction involves distraction in two opposite directions to gradually expand the bone or tissue. Both types of systems are used in orthopedic and craniofacial surgeries to correct deformities, fractures, or deficiencies in the craniofacial region, offering more tailored and effective treatment options for patients.</p></p>
