<p><ul><li>Single Face Board</li><li>Single Wall Board</li><li>Double Wall Board</li><li>Triple Wall Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated board market includes various types such as Single Face Board, Single Wall Board, Double Wall Board, and Triple Wall Board. Single Face Board consists of a single layer of fluting glued to one linerboard. Single Wall Board has a fluted layer sandwiched between two linerboards. Double Wall Board consists of two layers of fluting sandwiched between three linerboards. Triple Wall Board contains three layers of fluting sandwiched between four linerboards, making it the strongest and most durable option among the four types.</p></p>
