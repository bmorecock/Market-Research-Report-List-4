<p><p>Connected lighting refers to the integration of lighting systems with network control and Power-over-Ethernet technology. In network control, lighting fixtures are connected through a centralized network allowing for remote monitoring and control of individual fixtures or groups of fixtures. Power-over-Ethernet technology enables lighting fixtures to receive power and data through a single Ethernet cable, simplifying installation and operation. Both types of connected lighting offer increased energy efficiency, flexibility, and customization options for users in various environments such as commercial buildings, offices, and smart homes.</p></p>
