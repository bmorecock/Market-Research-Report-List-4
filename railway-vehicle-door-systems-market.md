<p><p>Railway vehicle door systems are available in three main types based on their operation - electrical, pneumatic, and manual. Electrical door systems are operated using electric motors, providing efficient and automated door opening and closing. Pneumatic systems use compressed air to operate doors, offering a reliable and energy-efficient option. Manual door systems are operated by passengers or train staff physically opening and closing the doors. Each type of system has its own benefits and features to cater to the specific requirements of railway vehicles.</p></p>
