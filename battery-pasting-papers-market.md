Cellulose pasting papers are made from wood pulp and are biodegradable, while fiberglass pasting papers are made from fiberglass fibers and are more durable and heat-resistant. Both types of pasting papers cater to different market needs, with cellulose pasting papers being more eco-friendly and fiberglass pasting papers offering enhanced performance in demanding battery applications.</p></p>
