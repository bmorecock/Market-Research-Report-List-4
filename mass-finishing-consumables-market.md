<p><p>Mass finishing consumables market types include deburring, pressure deburring, grinding, polishing, ball furnishing, cleaning & degreasing, descaling, corrosion & rust removal, and surface finish & refinement. Deburring involves removing sharp edges and burrs from metal parts, while pressure deburring uses pressurized water or air to achieve the same result. Grinding is the process of smoothing or shaping a surface using an abrasive wheel, while polishing involves giving a smooth and glossy finish to a surface. Ball furnishing uses small metal balls to improve surface smoothness, cleaning & degreasing removes dirt and oils from parts, descaling removes scale and rust from metal surfaces, and corrosion & rust removal removes corrosion and rust buildup. Surface finish & refinement focuses on enhancing the overall appearance and quality of the surface.</p></p>
