<p><p>Handheld Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) monitors are available in two main types - PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor. PID monitors are more sensitive and can detect a wider range of VOCs, making them suitable for various applications such as industrial hygiene and environmental monitoring. Metal-oxide Semiconductor monitors are more affordable and have a longer lifespan, making them a popular choice for general VOC monitoring in industries like manufacturing and construction. Both types of monitors play a crucial role in ensuring air quality and safety in various environments.</p></p>
