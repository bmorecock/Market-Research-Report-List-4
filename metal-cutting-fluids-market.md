<p><ul><li>Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Synthesis Metal Cutting Fluids</li><li>Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal Cutting Fluids market can be categorized into four types based on their composition: Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids, Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids, Synthesis Metal Cutting Fluids, and Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids. Emulsion metal cutting fluids are water-based and offer good lubrication. Semi-synthetic fluids are a blend of mineral oil and synthetic compounds, providing a balance between lubrication and cooling. Synthesis fluids are fully synthetic and offer excellent cooling properties. Neat oil metal cutting fluids are straight oils without any water content, suitable for heavy-duty cutting operations.</p></p>
