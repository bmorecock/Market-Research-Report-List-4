<p><p>The defibrillator devices market consists of two main types: Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillator (ICD) and External Cardioverter Defibrillator. ICDs are surgically implanted within the body to monitor heart rhythms and deliver electric shocks when needed. External Cardioverter Defibrillators are portable devices used in emergency situations to deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythms. Both types of defibrillator devices play crucial roles in treating life-threatening cardiac arrhythmias and are integral components of modern cardiac care.</p></p>
