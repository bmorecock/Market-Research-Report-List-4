<p><ul><li>Reflective Light Curtain Sensors</li><li>Diffuse Light Curtain Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light curtain sensors are used in various industrial applications and are available in different types. Reflective light curtain sensors use a transmitter and receiver to detect presence or absence of an object. Diffuse light curtain sensors have a single unit housing both transmitter and receiver, with the object reflecting the light back to the sensor. Reflective sensors are suitable for longer range applications, while diffuse sensors are more compact and cost-effective. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficiency in industrial environments.</p></p>
