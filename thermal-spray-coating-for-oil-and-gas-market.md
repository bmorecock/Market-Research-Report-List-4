<p><p>Thermal spray coating is a process used in the oil and gas industry to protect equipment from corrosion and wear. This process involves spraying a substrate material onto the surface of components using various methods such as cold spray, flame spray, plasma spray, and others. Cold spray uses kinetic energy to deposit particles, while flame spray involves melting and spraying a feedstock powder. Plasma spray uses a high-temperature plasma stream to coat surfaces. Each method offers unique advantages for different applications in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
