Tunnels car washing service involves an automated system that guides the car through a tunnel where it is washed using high-pressure equipment. Roll-over/in-bay car washing service consists of a stationary machine that moves over the parked car to clean it. Self-service car washing allows customers to wash their own cars using equipment and facilities provided at a designated location.</p></p>
