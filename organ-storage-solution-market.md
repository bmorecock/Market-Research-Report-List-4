<p><ul><li>Static Storage Solution</li><li>Perfusion Storage Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static Storage Solution refers to the traditional method of storing organs at low temperatures in cold storage units to preserve them for transplantation. Perfusion Storage Solution involves circulating a cold preservation solution through the organ to maintain its viability for longer periods, allowing for better preservation and potentially increasing the organ's suitability for transplantation. Both types of storage solutions are crucial in organ transplantation as they help maintain the organ's quality and prolong its preservation until it is ready for transplant.</p></p>
