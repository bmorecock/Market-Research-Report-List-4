<p><ul><li>Depthway</li><li>Lengthway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nylon knotless fishing nets are divided into two main market types based on the orientation of their construction: depthway and lengthway. Depthway nets are made with parallel strands running vertically, allowing for efficient fishing in deeper waters where vertical support is important. Lengthway nets, on the other hand, are constructed with strands running horizontally, making them well-suited for catching fish in shallower waters where horizontal spread is essential. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the fishing environment and target species.</p></p>
