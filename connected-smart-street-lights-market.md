<p><p>Connected street lights can be categorized into wired (Power Line Communication) and wireless markets. Wired street lights use power line communication technology to connect the lights to a central management system. On the other hand, wireless street lights rely on wireless communication protocols such as Zigbee or LoRa to establish connectivity. While wired street lights offer stable and reliable connections, wireless street lights provide more flexibility and scalability. Both types of connected street lights are gaining traction in the market for their ability to enhance energy efficiency and improve overall lighting management.</p></p>
