<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine SOx Scrubber System market consists of three main types: Open Loop Scrubbers, Closed Loop Scrubbers, and Hybrid Scrubbers. Open Loop Scrubbers use seawater to clean exhaust gases before releasing them back into the ocean. Closed Loop Scrubbers recirculate a liquid solution to remove pollutants and produce a waste byproduct. Hybrid Scrubbers combine features of both systems, offering greater flexibility in operation. Each type of scrubber has its advantages and limitations, catering to different needs and regulations within the maritime industry.</p></p>
