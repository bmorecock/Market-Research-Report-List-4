<p><p>Paramotor engines come in two main types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke. The 2-stroke engines are lighter, simpler, and typically more affordable than their 4-stroke counterparts. They have a power stroke every two strokes of the piston and are known for their high power-to-weight ratio. On the other hand, 4-stroke engines have a power stroke every four strokes, making them more fuel-efficient and environmentally friendly. They generally have smoother power delivery and are quieter than 2-stroke engines. Ultimately, the choice between the two types depends on individual preferences and specific needs.</p></p>
