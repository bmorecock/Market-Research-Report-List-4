<p><p>Oil and Gas Water Purification System Market includes membrane processes, thermal treatment, ion exchange, and capacitive deionization. Membrane processes involve filtering water through a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities. Thermal treatment uses heat to separate impurities from water. Ion exchange involves exchanging ions to remove impurities from water. Capacitive deionization uses electrical fields to remove ions from water. Each of these technologies plays a crucial role in purifying water for the oil and gas industry, ensuring environmental safety and efficiency.</p></p>
