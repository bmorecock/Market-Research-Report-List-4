<p><p>Product customization software can be categorized into two main market types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises software is installed and operated on the company's local servers, allowing for greater control and customization but requiring more maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is accessed and managed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for physical infrastructure. Both types cater to different business needs, with On-Premises focusing on security and control, while Cloud-Based prioritizes accessibility and ease of use.</p></p>
