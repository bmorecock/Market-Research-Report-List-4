<p><p>Petrochemical wastewater treatment equipment market includes four main types of treatment: biological treatment, ion exchange treatment, membrane treatment, and physico-chemical treatment. Biological treatment uses microorganisms to break down organic pollutants. Ion exchange treatment removes pollutants through chemical reactions with ions. Membrane treatment uses filters to separate pollutants from water. Physico-chemical treatment combines physical and chemical processes to treat wastewater. Each type of treatment is crucial in removing contaminants from petrochemical wastewater to ensure environmental protection and water quality.</p></p>
