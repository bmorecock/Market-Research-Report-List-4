<p><ul><li>Traditional POP</li><li>PSfcCSP</li><li>Through-Mold-Via</li><li>Exposed-die TMV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Package on package (PoP) technology allows two separate packages to be stacked on top of each other, creating a compact and high-performance integrated circuit. The traditional PoP market involves stacking memory on top of a processor. Package-on-package with stacked fine-pitch chip scale package (PSfcCSP) technology allows for even denser integration. Through-mold-via PoP involves embedding vias directly through the mold compound. Exposed-die TMV PoP exposes the dies on the bottom package, providing better thermal performance. Each market type offers unique advantages for different applications.</p></p>
