<p><ul><li>Cylindrical Support</li><li>Bushing Support</li><li>Conical Support</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-seismic support systems are designed to reduce the impact of earthquakes on buildings and structures. The market for these systems includes three main types: Cylindrical Support, Bushing Support, and Conical Support. Cylindrical Support systems utilize cylindrical shaped supports to absorb seismic forces. Bushing Support systems use bushings to dampen vibrations and provide stability. Conical Support systems employ conical shaped supports to distribute and minimize seismic forces. These different types of support systems offer varying levels of protection and are chosen based on the specific needs of the structure.</p></p>
