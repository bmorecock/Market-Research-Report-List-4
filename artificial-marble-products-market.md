<p><p>Artificial marble products market consists of various types such as cement artificial marble, resin type artificial marble, composite artificial marble, and sintered artificial marble. Cement artificial marble is made using cement and marble powder, offering durability and cost-effectiveness. Resin type artificial marble is produced using resin and marble dust, offering a glossy finish. Composite artificial marble combines natural marble with resin for enhanced strength. Sintered artificial marble is made by compressing natural marble chips with resin at high temperatures, providing a durable and low-maintenance option.</p></p>
