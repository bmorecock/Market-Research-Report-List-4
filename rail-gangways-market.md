<p><p>Rail gangways are essential for safe passage between train cars. There are two main types in the market - single piece rail gangways and two piece rail gangways. Single piece gangways are fixed and do not fold or retract, providing a continuous passage between cars. Two piece gangways are hinged in the middle, allowing them to fold and retract when not in use. Both types offer convenience and safety for passengers and crew when moving between train cars.</p></p>
