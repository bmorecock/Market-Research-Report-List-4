<p><p>Pneumatic assembly tools are divided into two main types: fixture and handheld. Fixture tools are stationary devices typically used for large-scale manufacturing operations where precision and consistency are crucial. They are designed to be mounted onto workbenches or assembly lines for efficient production. Handheld tools, on the other hand, are portable devices that offer more flexibility and mobility for tasks that require manual dexterity and versatility. Both types of tools play a vital role in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
