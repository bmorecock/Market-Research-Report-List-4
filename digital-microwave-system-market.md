<p><p>The digital microwave system market consists of three main types of microwave radios: Hybrid Microwave Radio, Packet Microwave Radio, and TDM (Time Division Multiplexing) Microwave Radio. </p><p>Hybrid Microwave Radio combines the benefits of both TDM and packet technologies, offering high capacity and flexibility. Packet Microwave Radio transmits data in small packets, providing efficient and scalable data transmission. TDM Microwave Radio uses time slots to prioritize voice and data traffic for efficient communication. Each type caters to specific network requirements, offering unique features and benefits for various applications.</p></p>
