<p><p>The cordless band saw market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal cordless band saws. Vertical cordless band saws are ideal for cutting curves and intricate shapes due to their up-and-down motion, making them popular in woodworking and metalworking industries. Horizontal cordless band saws are more suited for straight cuts and precise angles, making them a preferred choice for cutting pipes, tubes, and other materials in industries like plumbing and construction. Both types offer portability and convenience without the restriction of cords.</p></p>
