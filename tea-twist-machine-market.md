<p><p>Tea twist machines can be categorized into two main market types: manual twist and mechanical twist machines. Manual twist machines are operated by hand, requiring physical effort from the user to twist the tea leaves. These machines are simple, cost-effective, and suitable for small-scale operations. On the other hand, mechanical twist machines are powered by electricity or other mechanical sources, automating the twisting process. These machines are faster, more efficient, and ideal for large-scale tea processing facilities.</p></p>
