<p><p>Eco paper market types include Recycled Post-Consumer Waste (RPD) and Recycled Pre-Consumer Waste (RBD). RPD refers to paper made from materials that have already been used by consumers and recovered for recycling. RBD, on the other hand, consists of paper waste generated during the manufacturing process that is recycled. These types of eco paper help reduce the demand for new raw materials and decrease environmental impact. Other markets may include specialty eco papers made from alternative fibers such as bamboo or hemp.</p></p>
