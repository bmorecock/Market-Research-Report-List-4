<p><p>Blood sensor market can be categorized into minimally invasive and non-invasive types based on the method of blood sample collection. Minimally invasive sensors require a small amount of blood obtained through a finger prick or venous puncture. Non-invasive sensors, on the other hand, do not require any blood sample collection and instead measure blood parameters through external means such as skin sensors or optical sensors. Both types of sensors offer accurate and convenient blood parameter monitoring for various healthcare applications.</p></p>
