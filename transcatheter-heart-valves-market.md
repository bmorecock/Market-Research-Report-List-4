<p><p>Transcatheter Heart Valves Market includes Transcatheter Aortic Valve, Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve, and Transcatheter Mitral Valve. Transcatheter Aortic Valve is used to treat aortic valve stenosis, a condition where the heart's aortic valve narrows and restricts blood flow. Transcatheter Pulmonary Valve is utilized for treating congenital heart defects that affect the pulmonary valve. Transcatheter Mitral Valve is designed for patients with mitral regurgitation, a condition where the heart's mitral valve doesn't close properly, leading to blood leakage. Each type of valve serves a specific purpose in addressing various heart conditions.</p></p>
