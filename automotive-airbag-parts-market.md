<p><p>The automotive airbag parts market consists of various types of components designed to ensure the safety of vehicle occupants during a crash. These include airbag cushions, flexible fabric bags, inflation modules, and impact sensors. Airbag cushions are the actual protective devices that inflate upon impact, while flexible fabric bags provide the containment for the expanding gas. Inflation modules control the release of gas to inflate the airbag, and impact sensors detect the force of a collision to trigger the airbag deployment.</p></p>
