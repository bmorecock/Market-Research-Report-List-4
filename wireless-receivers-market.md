<p><p>Wireless receivers can be classified into two main types based on the method of power transfer: inductive coupling and capacitive coupling. Inductive coupling wireless power receivers use electromagnetic fields to transfer power wirelessly, commonly used in Qi wireless charging technology. Capacitive coupling wireless power receivers, on the other hand, rely on electric fields to transfer power wirelessly, making them suitable for short-range and fast-charging applications. Both types of wireless receivers play a crucial role in enabling convenient and efficient wireless charging solutions in various industries.</p></p>
