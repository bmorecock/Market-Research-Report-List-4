Total Distillation Testing is used to determine the fractions of crude oil at different boiling points. Freeze Point Testing measures the lowest temperature at which the oil turns solid. Smoke Point Testing assesses the combustion qualities of the oil. Total Sulfur Testing measures the amount of sulfur present in the oil. Other tests may include viscosity, density, and flash point testing.</p></p>
