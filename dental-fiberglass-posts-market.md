<p><p>Dental fiberglass posts are commonly available in three different types on the market: tapered, parallel, and other. Tapered posts have a conical shape, making them suitable for teeth with varying root canal diameters. Parallel posts have a consistent diameter along their entire length, providing stability in straight root canals. Other posts may include variations such as serrated or ribbed designs for enhanced retention. Dentists select the appropriate post type based on the specific characteristics of the patient's tooth and root canal.</p></p>
