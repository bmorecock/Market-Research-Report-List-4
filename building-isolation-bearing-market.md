<p><p>Building isolation bearing market is segmented into two main types: Elastomeric Bearings and Sliding Bearings. Elastomeric Bearings use rubber pads to absorb and isolate vibration and movement in a building, providing flexibility and damping properties. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring structural stability, safety, and protection against seismic activity and environmental factors in buildings.</p></p>
