<p><p>Silicon Carbide ingots are available in different types, with N-type and Semi-insulated Type being the most common in the market. N-type Silicon Carbide ingots have a high concentration of electrons, allowing them to be used in applications requiring high conductivity. On the other hand, Semi-insulated Type Silicon Carbide ingots have a lower concentration of impurities, which results in higher resistivity and is suitable for high-frequency power devices. Both types cater to various industries and applications based on their specific conductivity and resistivity characteristics.</p></p>
