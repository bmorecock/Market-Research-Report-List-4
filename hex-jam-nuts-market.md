<p><p>Hex jam nuts are commonly used in applications where vibrations may cause conventional nuts to loosen. Left-hand hex jam nuts are designed to be tightened in a counterclockwise direction, while right-hand hex jam nuts are tightened in a clockwise direction. Both types of nuts serve the same purpose of securing bolts and preventing them from coming loose due to vibrations. The choice between left-hand and right-hand hex jam nuts depends on the specific application and the direction in which the bolt will be rotating.</p></p>
