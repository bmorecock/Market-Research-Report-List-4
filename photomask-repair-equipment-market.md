<p><p>Photomask repair equipment market offers three main types of technologies - Laser Technology, Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology, and Nanomachining Technology. Laser Technology uses laser beams to repair defects on photomasks. Focused Ion Beam (FIB) Technology uses a focused beam of ions to remove or deposit material on the photomask. Nanomachining Technology utilizes small-scale cutting tools to repair photomasks at the nanometer level. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and functionality of photomasks used in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
