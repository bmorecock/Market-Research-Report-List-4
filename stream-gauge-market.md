<p><p>Stream gauges are devices used to measure the flow rate of liquid or gas in a pipe. There are several types of stream gauges available in the market such as Positive Displacement, Ultrasonic, Turbine, Magnetic, Coriolis, and Vortex. Positive Displacement stream gauges use a rotating mechanism to measure flow, Ultrasonic gauges use sound waves, Turbine gauges use a spinning rotor, Magnetic gauges use electromagnetic technology, Coriolis gauges rely on the Coriolis effect, and Vortex gauges measure flow by detecting vortices created by the flowing fluid.</p></p>
