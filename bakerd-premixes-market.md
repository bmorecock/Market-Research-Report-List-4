<p><ul><li>Complete mix</li><li>Dough-base mix</li><li>Dough concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Baker's premixes are divided into three main types, which are complete mix, dough-base mix, and dough concentrates. </p><p>Complete mix includes all the necessary ingredients to create a finished product, requiring minimal addition of other components.</p><p>Dough-base mix provides the essential dry or liquid ingredients for making dough, such as flour, sugar, and spices, needing the addition of water or other wet ingredients.</p><p>Dough concentrates are highly concentrated mixes that require the addition of water and yeast to make dough, reducing the overall production cost.</p></p>
