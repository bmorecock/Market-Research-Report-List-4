<p><strong>The Nasal Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bioresorbable</li><li>Not Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nasal dressing market can be categorized into two types: bioresorbable and not absorbable. Bioresorbable nasal dressings are made of materials that can be gradually broken down and absorbed by the body over time, reducing the need for removal procedures. On the other hand, non-absorbable nasal dressings are made of materials that remain in place until they are removed by a healthcare provider. Both types serve the purpose of providing support and protection to the nasal cavity during the healing process after nasal surgeries.</p></p>
