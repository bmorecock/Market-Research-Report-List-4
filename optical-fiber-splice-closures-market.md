<p><ul><li>Dome Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fiber splice closures are classified into two main types: Dome Type and Horizontal Type. Dome Type closures are dome-shaped and designed for aerial, wall-mounted, or buried applications. They provide protection for fiber splices in harsh environments and are easy to install and maintain. Horizontal Type closures are rectangular-shaped and are typically used in underground or direct burial applications. They are known for their compact design and ability to accommodate a large number of fiber connections. Both types offer different benefits depending on the specific installation requirements.</p></p>
