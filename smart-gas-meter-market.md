<p><p>Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) is a type of smart gas meter market where meters automatically collect consumption data and transmit it to a central database for billing purposes. Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) market goes a step further by enabling two-way communication between the meter and utility provider, allowing for real-time monitoring and control of gas consumption. Both AMR and AMI technologies are key players in the transformation of traditional gas metering systems into efficient, data-driven solutions for utilities and consumers.</p></p>
