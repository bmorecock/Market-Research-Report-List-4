<p><p>Pickling chemicals market can be categorized into two types: aqueous solution and concentrated solution. Aqueous solution pickling chemicals are primarily water-based solutions that are diluted and ready-to-use for pickling applications. On the other hand, concentrated solution pickling chemicals are more potent and need to be diluted with water before use. Both types of pickling chemicals play a crucial role in removing rust and scale from metal surfaces, preparing them for subsequent processes such as plating or coating.</p></p>
