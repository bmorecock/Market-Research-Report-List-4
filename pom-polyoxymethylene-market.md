<p><p>POM, also known as Polyoxymethylene, is a type of engineering plastic widely used in various industries. There are two main market types of POM: POM-H and POM-C. POM-H, also called homopolymer, offers high stiffness and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring mechanical precision. Meanwhile, POM-C, or copolymer, provides improved thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for parts exposed to harsh environments. Both types of POM offer excellent wear resistance, low friction, and dimensional stability, making them valuable materials in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
