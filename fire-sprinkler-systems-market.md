<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Dry Pipe Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Pre-Action Fire Sprinkler Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire Sprinkler Systems Market Types include Wet Pipe, Dry Pipe, Deluge, and Pre-Action Systems. Wet Pipe Systems are the most common, with water always stored in the pipes ready to be released. Dry Pipe Systems use pressurized air or nitrogen to hold back water until a fire is detected. Deluge Systems release large amounts of water simultaneously, often used in high-hazard areas. Pre-Action Systems combine aspects of both wet and dry systems, requiring two triggers before water is released for added protection against false alarms.</p></p>
