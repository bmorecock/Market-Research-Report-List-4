<p><p>Conforming bandages are available in two main types: sterile and non-sterile. Sterile conforming bandages are individually packaged and guaranteed to be free from microorganisms, making them suitable for use in medical settings where infection control is crucial. Non-sterile conforming bandages, on the other hand, are not packaged in a sterile manner and may be used for non-medical purposes such as supporting joints or securing dressings. Both types of bandages provide compression and support to injured areas.</p></p>
