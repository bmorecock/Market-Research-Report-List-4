<p><p>The Casting Polyurethane market can be classified into three main types based on the type of prepolymer used: Polyester Base Prepolymer, Polyether Base Prepolymer, and Polycaprolactone Base Prepolymer. Polyester Base Prepolymer offers excellent mechanical properties and solvent resistance, making it ideal for applications requiring high durability. Polyether Base Prepolymer provides superior flexibility and low-temperature performance, making it suitable for applications requiring impact resistance. Polycaprolactone Base Prepolymer offers a unique combination of properties including biodegradability, making it suitable for environmentally friendly applications.</p></p>
