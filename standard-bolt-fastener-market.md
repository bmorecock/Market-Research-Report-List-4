<p><p>In the standard bolt market, two common types are half screw bolts and full screw bolts. Half screw bolts have a partially threaded shaft, allowing for easy installation and removal. They are commonly used in applications where frequent disassembly is required. Full screw bolts have a fully threaded shaft, providing better grip and stability. They are typically used in applications where a strong and secure fastening connection is necessary. Both types cater to different needs in the fastener market.</p></p>
