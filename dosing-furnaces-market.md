<p><p>Dosing furnaces are used in metal casting industries for precise and controlled dosing of molten metal. There are two main types of dosing furnaces on the market: electrical heating system and electro-pneumatic dose system. Electrical heating system uses electricity to heat the molten metal, providing a stable and efficient heating process. On the other hand, electro-pneumatic dose system utilizes a combination of pneumatic pressure and electricity to control the dosing of molten metal, offering improved accuracy and control over the dosing process.</p></p>
