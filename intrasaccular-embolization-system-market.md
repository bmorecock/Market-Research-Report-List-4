<p><strong>The Intrasaccular Embolization System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embolic Coils</li><li>Woven EndoBridge (WEB)</li><li>Medina Embolization Device</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intrasaccular embolization systems are used to treat intracranial aneurysms. The market includes embolic coils, which are coil-shaped devices placed within the aneurysm to promote clot formation and prevent rupture. The Woven EndoBridge (WEB) device creates a scaffold inside the aneurysm to promote clotting. The Medina Embolization Device is a novel system that combines a coil with a stent-like structure to provide effective occlusion. Other systems in the market include flow diverters, liquid embolics, and Onyx coils.</p></p>
