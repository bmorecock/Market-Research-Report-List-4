<p><p>Horizontal paddle mixers are available in two main market types: Continuous and Discontinuous. Continuous paddle mixers are designed for high-volume, continuous mixing processes where material is continuously fed into the mixer and discharged at the other end. On the other hand, Discontinuous paddle mixers are meant for batch processing, where materials are loaded in batches, mixed, and then discharged. Both types cater to different industries and applications based on the specific mixing requirements and production processes.</p></p>
