<p><ul><li>Placer Mining</li><li>Sluicing</li><li>Gold Panning</li><li>Dredging</li><li>Hard-Rock Mining</li><li>Rocker Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gold mining market involves various types of methods for extracting gold from the earth. Placer mining is the process of using water to separate gold nuggets from sand or gravel. Sluicing involves directing water through a sluice box to catch gold particles. Gold panning is done by swirling water and gravel in a pan to isolate gold flakes. Dredging involves using a large machine to suck up gravel and extract gold. Hard-rock mining involves extracting gold from solid rock formations. Rocker box is a hand-operated device used for separating gold from dirt and gravel.</p></p>
