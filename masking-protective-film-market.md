<p><strong>The Masking Protective Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Masking Protective Film</li><li>Liquid Masking Protective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Masking protective film is categorized into two types: solid masking protective film and liquid masking protective film. Solid masking protective film is a peelable film that adheres to surfaces to protect them from damage during processes like painting or sandblasting. Liquid masking protective film, on the other hand, is a liquid that can be applied over surfaces and dries into a protective film layer. Both types offer protection against scratches, dirt, and debris, making them popular choices in industries such as automotive, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
