<p><p>Printing inks for digital textiles come in various types to cater to different requirements. Reactive dye inks are known for their excellent color fastness and vibrant hues. Acidic inks are used for printing on silk and wool fabrics due to their compatibility with these fibers. Paint inks are ideal for achieving a textured or hand-painted look on textiles. Dispersion inks offer good coverage and adhesion, while sublimation inks are used for printing on polyester fabrics by transferring inks from a solid to a gas state.</p></p>
