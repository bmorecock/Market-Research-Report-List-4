<p><p>The RF Lesion Generator market consists of two main types: Conventional Radio Frequency (RF) and Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF). Conventional RF involves the continuous delivery of radiofrequency energy to create lesions on nerve tissue for pain management. In contrast, PRF delivers bursts of energy at adjustable intervals to provide pain relief without damaging surrounding healthy tissue. These technologies offer healthcare providers different options for treating chronic pain conditions, with each method offering unique benefits and outcomes for patients.</p></p>
