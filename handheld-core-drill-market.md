<p><strong>The Handheld Core Drill Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Core Drill</li><li>Wet Core Drill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld core drills are commonly classified into two main types based on their operation: dry core drills and wet core drills. Dry core drills are used without any water coolant and are suitable for drilling into materials like concrete, brick, and stone. Wet core drills, on the other hand, use water coolant to lubricate and cool the drill bit during operation, making them ideal for drilling into harder materials like reinforced concrete and granite. Both types of core drills have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
