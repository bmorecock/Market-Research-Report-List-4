<p><p>Wafer permanent bonders can be categorized into two main types: semi-automated and fully-automated bonders. Semi-automated bonders require some manual intervention during the bonding process, while fully-automated bonders operate without the need for human interaction once the process is initiated. Semi-automated bonders are typically more cost-effective and offer greater flexibility, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, fully-automated bonders are ideal for high-volume production environments where efficiency and precision are paramount.</p></p>
