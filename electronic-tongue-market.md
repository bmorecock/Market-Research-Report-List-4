<p><strong>The Electronic Tongue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Type</li><li>Amperometric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Potentiometric type electronic tongues measure changes in potential between electrodes in response to different ions or compounds present in a sample. This type of electronic tongue is sensitive to changes in concentration and can provide information about taste profiles and quality of the sample. </p><p>Amperometric type electronic tongues measure changes in current resulting from electrochemical reactions between compounds in a sample and the electrodes. This type of electronic tongue is more sensitive to specific compounds and can be used for in-depth analysis and discrimination of samples.</p></p>
