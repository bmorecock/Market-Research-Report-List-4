<p><p>Tire testing systems are essential for evaluating the performance and durability of tires. There are different types of tire testing systems, including Flat-Trac, Rolling Resistance, and Tread Wear markets. The Flat-Trac system measures the traction and braking abilities of tires on a flat surface. Rolling Resistance testing assesses the energy efficiency of tires by measuring the force required for them to roll. Tread Wear testing evaluates the longevity of tires by simulating wear and tear over time. These systems help manufacturers develop high-quality tires for various applications.</p></p>
