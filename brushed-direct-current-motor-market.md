<p><p>Brushed Direct Current (DC) Motors are classified into four main types based on their winding configurations: Series Wound Brush DC Motors, Permanent Magnet Brush DC Motors, Shunt Wound Brush DC Motors, and Compound Wound Brush DC Motors. Series Wound motors have high starting torque and are used for applications requiring high power. Permanent Magnet motors are compact and efficient, ideal for portable devices. Shunt Wound motors offer good speed regulation and are commonly used in industrial applications. Compound Wound motors combine the characteristics of both series and shunt wound motors for versatile performance.</p></p>
