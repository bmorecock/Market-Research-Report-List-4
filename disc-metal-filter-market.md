<p><ul><li>Wire Mesh</li><li>Laser Drilled Holes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disc metal filters are available in two main types: wire mesh filters and laser drilled hole filters. Wire mesh filters are created by weaving together strands of metal to form a mesh, providing filtration through the openings in the mesh. On the other hand, laser drilled hole filters are manufactured by using laser technology to create precise holes in a metal disc, allowing for more controlled filtration. Both types of filters offer effective filtration capabilities for various industrial applications.</p></p>
