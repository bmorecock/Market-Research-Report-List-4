<p><p>Silica slurry market can be divided into two main types - Micron Grade and Nano Grade. Micron Grade silica slurry consists of particles that are larger in size, typically ranging from 1 to 10 microns. On the other hand, Nano Grade silica slurry contains particles that are much smaller, in the nano-size range. The difference in particle size between the two grades affects their applications and properties, with Nano Grade silica slurry being preferred for industries that require materials with higher surface area and increased reactivity.</p></p>
